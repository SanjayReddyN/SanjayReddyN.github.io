    My 5 Favorite Albums Are as Follows
  1. Rodeo : Travis Scott

    One of Travis Scott's first albums, it's definetely my favorite of his, the beats and variation mid song in many of these songs make them very fun to listen to.

  2. The Division Bell : Pink Floyd

    Probably my favorite concept album of all time, Pink Floyd are known for how to naturally make songs flow into each other, which great instruments and vocals

  3. MBDTF : Kanye West

    Although Kanye West isn't a great human being, his music has always been top class in the hip hop industry, due to albums like these. Every single one of these songs are incredible, with great beats, lyrics and features.

  4. Circles: Mac Miller

    Mac Miller's first post mortem album, published and finished by his producer. It was my introduction to Mac Miller. While it's generally a slower paced and sadder album, I really connected with it during the pandemic, I used to listen to it on repeat, it holds a special place in my heart.

  5. Mr. Morale and the big steppers: Kendrick Lamar

    This was Kendrick's first album in a long time, and he knocked it out of the part, with great beats and music, but most all, it shows him coming to terms with his mental health issues, and he discusses different aspects of life seldom touched upon in the hip hop industry.

My top songs from 2021, mostly Mac Miller.
